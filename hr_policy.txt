# Company HR Policy Handbook 2025

## 1. Leave Policy
### 1.1 Annual Leave
- All full-time employees are entitled to 27 days of paid annual leave per year.
- Leave must be applied for at least 3 days in advance via the HR portal.
- Unused leave can be carried forward up to 10 days to the next year.

### 1.2 Sick Leave
- Employees get 10 days of sick leave annually.
- A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days.

### 1.3 Casual Leave
- 8 days of casual leave are available for personal matters.

### 1.4 Maternity & Paternity Leave
- Maternity Leave: 26 weeks of paid leave.
- Paternity Leave: 2 weeks of paid leave.

## 2. Remote Work Policy
- Employees are allowed to work remotely for up to 2 days a week (Hybrid Model).
- Core working hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
- Approval from the reporting manager is mandatory for extended remote work.
- Employees must ensure a stable internet connection and a quiet workspace.

## 3. Employee Benefits
### 3.1 Health Insurance
- Comprehensive health insurance covers the employee, spouse, and two children.
- Coverage limit: ₹5,00,000 per annum.
- Includes dental and vision checkups once a year.

### 3.2 Learning & Development
- The company provides a learning allowance of ₹20,000 per year for certifications and courses.
- Access to Udemy and Coursera enterprise accounts is free for all employees.

### 3.3 Wellness
- Free gym membership is available at partnered centers.
- Mental health counseling sessions are available upon request (confidential).

## 4. Code of Conduct
- Professional behavior is expected at all times.
- The company has a zero-tolerance policy for harassment and discrimination.
- Dress code is "Smart Casual" from Monday to Thursday, and "Casual" on Fridays.

## 5. Performance Reviews
- Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually (June and December).
- Promotions and salary hikes are decided based on the annual performance score.
- "Star Performer" awards are given quarterly with a bonus of ₹10,000.

## 6. Travel Policy
- Domestic Travel: Economy class flights for travel > 6 hours; otherwise, AC train/bus.
- International Travel: Economy class.
- Daily Allowance: ₹2,000 per day for food and incidentals during travel.
- Accommodation: 4-star hotels or equivalent (capped at ₹5,000/night).

## 7. IT & Security Policy
- Use of personal devices for work is permitted only after IT security clearance (BYOD).
- Data breaches must be reported immediately to the CISO.
- Social media usage is restricted during core working hours.

## 8. Termination & Notice Period
- Notice period for resignation is 2 months.
- Buyout of notice period is at the discretion of the management.
- Termination for cause (e.g., fraud, harassment) is immediate without severance.
